Our Story

Founded in 2025, Bean Boutique started as a small coffee pop-up shop and has grown into a full-fledged coffee community hub. We’re passionate about brewing excellence and sourcing the best beans from around the world.

What We Believe

  • Supporting fair trade farmers
  • Serving top-tier specialty coffee
  • Hosting events and workshops for coffee lovers

Meet Our Team

We’re a team of coffee nerds, baristas, roasters, and dreamers based in Myanmar. Stop by and say hi!
